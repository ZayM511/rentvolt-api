## 8. Your California Privacy Rights (CCPA / CPRA)

California residents have the following rights, subject to verification of identity:

1. **Right to Know** what personal information we have collected, the sources, the purposes, and the categories of recipients (§ 1798.100, 1798.110).
2. **Right to Delete** personal information we have collected from you (§ 1798.105), subject to enumerated exceptions (e.g., to complete a transaction, comply with legal obligations, or exercise free-speech rights).
3. **Right to Correct** inaccurate personal information (§ 1798.106).
4. **Right to Opt Out of Sale or Share** of personal information (§ 1798.120). We do not sell or share personal information, but you may confirm this preference at `/legal/do-not-sell`.
5. **Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information** (§ 1798.121). We do not collect sensitive personal information other than as reasonably necessary to provide the Service.
6. **Right to Non-Discrimination** for exercising your rights (§ 1798.125).
7. **Right to Appeal** a denial of a prior request.

**How to exercise these rights.** Submit a verified request at `/privacy-request` or by emailing `legal@groundworklabs.io`. We will respond within 45 days, with a one-time 45-day extension when reasonably necessary. We will verify identity by matching the request to information we maintain (e.g., email address on file) and, where appropriate, by asking you to respond from the email on file.

**Authorized agents.** You may designate an agent to submit requests on your behalf. The agent must provide signed authorization; we may require you to verify your identity directly.

**Appeals.** If we deny your request, you may appeal by replying to our denial within 30 days. We will respond to appeals within 45 days.

## 9. Children
