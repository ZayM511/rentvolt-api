# Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

**Version:** 2026.04.17
**Effective Date:** April 17, 2026

## Our position

**Groundwork Labs LLC does not sell or share your personal information**, as those terms are defined under the California Consumer Privacy Act as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CCPA/CPRA, Cal. Civ. Code § 1798.100 et seq.). In particular:

- We do not exchange personal information with third parties for monetary or other valuable consideration.
- We do not disclose personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
- We honor Global Privacy Control (GPC) browser signals as an opt-out-of-sale/share preference.

## Your right to confirm this preference

If you would like to affirmatively record your preference not to sell or share your personal information, submit a request on our Privacy Request page:

**→ [/privacy-request](/privacy-request)**

Select "Opt out of sale / share of my personal information" as the request type. We will record the preference, respond within 15 business days to confirm, and maintain the preference for at least 12 months.

## Authorized agents

You may designate an authorized agent to submit this request on your behalf by providing the agent's signed authorization and sufficient identity information to verify your identity.

## Related rights

California residents have additional rights described in our [Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy), including the rights to know, delete, correct, limit use of sensitive personal information, appeal a denial, and receive non-discriminatory treatment.
